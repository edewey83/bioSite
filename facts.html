The Fantastic Person That Is Paul Inglehart

Interesting Facts

  • He has spent 3 summers in Russia
  • He has lived and traveled all over the world.
  • He holds 3 World Records for Gaming. All three pertain to Jurassic Park Games.
  • He Collect Dinosaurs of all kinds and sometimes resells them to others who enjoy Jurassic Park collectables.
